      A zine is a booklet
      made by a transgender

      any other options.

      information.

      a piece of information.
      A zine is, specifically,
      a chunk of information.
      It is made of + 7 smaller
      chunks of information.

    • The Theory of Memes

      Before it was rare Soyjaks, the meme was a serious theory by Dawkins '76. He named memetics following genetics, including select    tion. But his evolutionary metaphor didn't even apply to evolution. Don't get me wrong, I like his theory (and evolution.) But Dawkins called a meme a unit of culture. Sounds squishy, doesn't it ? Let's call it instead a chunk of information. Even better, a mirrorable chunk of information : one that can be easily repeated..

    THE FORMAT (that functions)

    • I. A zine is one page with zevenish parts.
    • II. A zine is black and white.
    • III. A zine is cheap or free.
    • IV. A zine photocopies easily.

    THE FILLING (that fucks)

    • A zine does not sell cheeseburgers.
    • A zine is small, below and against.
